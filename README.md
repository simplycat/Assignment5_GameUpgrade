# 🏚️ The Haunted Mansion

**A C++ OOP Text-Based Adventure**

You wake up with no memory in a long-abandoned Victorian mansion. The air is heavy. Portraits watch you from the walls. Somewhere inside are three relics that belonged to your ancestors — the *secret key*, the *ancient book*, and the *family heirloom*. Recover them and you might escape with your life.

The house doesn’t want to let you go.

## The Story
Trapped with nothing but your wits, you must explore the mansion, gather the relics that hold your family’s lost secrets, and unlock the exit in the Grand Hallway. But the mansion fights back. Evil dwarves lurk in the shadows, ready to steal your treasures and your life. Three lives are all you have — use them wisely. You must explore the mansion's rooms, gather the three sacred relics, and find your way back to the Grand Hallway to unlock the exit before the house claims you forever.

## Rooms
- **The Grand Hallway** — The heart of the mansion, where your journey begins and ends.
- **The Ancient Library** — Dusty shelves hide the key and ancestral secrets.
- **The Opulent Parlor** — Faded luxury conceals the ancient book… and an evil dwarf.
- **The Dark Basement** — A deadly trap. Enter at your own risk.

## New Features
- **3 Lives** — You get three lives. Lose them, and it’s game over.
- **Protection Spells** — Hidden ancestral wards you can find by searching. They shield you from death, one time use only.
- **Evil Dwarves** — They attack, try to steal your items, and can be defeated with protection spells.
- **Smart Inventory** — Shows your relics, spells, lives remaining, and how many dwarves you’ve taken down.

## How to Play
- go north / north / n (also south, east, west)
- look — Examine your surroundings
- take key / take book / take heirloom
- search — Hunt for hidden protection spells
- inventory / inv / i — Check your items, lives, spells, and dwarf kills
- exit - give up and quit the game

**Win Condition:** Collect all three items and successfully `go south` from the Grand Hallway while you still have lives left.

**Lose Condition:** Run out of lives.
